The Ideal Home View On…

Striped Upholstery

We’ve heard the experts explain why it’s becoming popular – so, do we think this microtrend is here to stay?

Deputy Editor Ginevra Benedetti weighs in…

“ Stripes are a truly timeless design – one that can straddle both traditional and contemporary styles – so it stands to reason that this trend is a lasting one. However, upholstered furniture is expensive and you really have to be invested in the look to make it worthy of the spend. So in my opinion, the success of this trend is ultimately down to personal choice. I wouldn’t risk the financial plunge on striped living room seating in my home – I’d just be worried that I’d want to change it before too long. If, however, it’s something you really crave, always trust your gut and go with what you love! ”
