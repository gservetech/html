Quick takeaways

  • If you need rigid columns: use table-layout: fixed + set widths (like first column 40%).
  • For long content inside tables: add overflow-wrap: anywhere on cells.
  • For lists: choose list-style-position intentionally; use ::marker for simple styling, or custom bullets for perfect alignment.
