Beat the Heat With

Serve cider with grilled and spicy dishes for an effortless summertime combo.

Craft cider is BBQ’s BFF. The acidity and sweetness of cider cools off and balances the heat from Mexican food, barbecue sauce, grilled meats and fish — you name it, there’s a perfect cider pairing for it. This summer, toss some cans of cider on ice and surprise your guests with one or more of these tasty, tried-and-true cider and food pairings.

Mexican Food

Think burritos, enchiladas, or nachos piled with jalapeños. This crisp cider takes the edge off.

Pair it with: POMMIES ORIGINAL. Made from the fresh-pressed juice of five apple varieties, its semi-sweet flavour profile dances between bright acidity and natural fruit sweetness.

Grilled Fish Tacos

Grilled fish tacos topped with mango salsa or tempura-battered prawns. A tart and bubbly hopped cider provides a welcome contrast.

Pair it with: COFFIN RIDGE DRY HOPPED CIDER. A great beer alternative with a balance of Ontario apples and aromatic hops—crisp and refreshing for al fresco meals.

Look for the Drink ON Apples logo. It guarantees the cider you’re choosing is crafted by Ontario cider makers using 100% Ontario-grown apples.
