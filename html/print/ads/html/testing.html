Make the main action at least 3x more obvious. Example below: primary (48px), secondary (40px), tertiary (36px).

Quick Checklist

  • [ ] Can someone understand the page in 5 seconds?
  • [ ] Are headlines over 60px using negative tracking?
  • [ ] Do nested rounded corners follow the formula?
  • [ ] Is all spacing a multiple of 8?
  • [ ] Are backgrounds tinted (not pure black/white)?
  • [ ] Do buttons explain what happens when clicked?
  • [ ] Have I tested at least 2 variations?
